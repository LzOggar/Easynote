About the project

Easynote is an encrypted notebook. It dedicated to privacy. For example, you can use Easynote to keep your secrets safe or keep your work safe. You are the only one who has access to your data. Easynote is very simple to use.

Applications used

Easynote is using followings plug-in :

  • Django
  • Bootstrap
  • HighCharts
  • Tinymce

More informations

Easynote uses the following mechanism to keep your data safe :

when you create an account via the register page. We will generate a master_key derived from your password with pbkdf2_hmac function then we will stock the master_key in your session browser. Then, you will automatically logged.

When you are logged, you have access to the dashboard where you can create new note via New note feature. Then, you type the name and content of the note and you save it. We will create an random key of 32 bytes then we will encrypt the content of your note with this key and AES CBC algorithm.

Finally, your key will be encrypt with your master_key and AES CBC algorithm. The encrypted content and the encrypted key will be saved in database.
